Enlightening through reading

Improving society by encouraging reading

We at Daani club devised the idea at a point of time when we wanted to donate certain books and stationery but realized that there are certain challenges in doing so – mainly uncertainty of where to donate the material and time constraints. Upon conversing with other individuals, we realized that these problems were more widespread than we initially forecasted and that a lot of people who wanted to donate old books weren’t doing so. We would like to make the reading experience more accessible by helping smoothen the process of redistribution of reading material by providing easy collection methods for donors as well as a steady flow of material for our partners.

Cause

“An investment in knowledge pays the best interest” -Benjamin Franklin

Daani Club is a non-profit NGO that is primarily focused on education in India. We at Daani Club have a vision that by 2030 India will achieve close to 100% literacy and our mission is to do our utmost to help the nation strive towards that goal. At Daani Club, we aim to help collect as many books as we can from willing donors (= Daani club members?) and redirect them towards our partner organizations, who in turn use the books to educate and facilitate indigent sections of society.
